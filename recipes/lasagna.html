Lasagna

Description

Your typical meat lasagna. Created by John Chandler and found on allrecipes.com. It takes a lot of time to create this- but the results are well-worth it.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps to Make

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
