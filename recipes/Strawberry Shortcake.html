Strawberry Shortcake

A delicacy consisting of fresh stawberries, the strawberry shortcake is a healthier option for dessert instead of the sugar-infested chocolate cake. Hard to make, but it will be all the sweeter once it's done.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups of Strawberries
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Half-and-half
  • Whipped Heavy Cream

How to Make Stawberry Shortcake

  1. Prep the berries: Slice the strawberries and cover them with white sugar.
  2. Make the batter: Combine the dry ingredients, then cut in the butter until the mixture is crumbly. Add the egg and half-and-half and stir until combined.
  3. Bake the cake: Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake until golden brown.
  4. Assemble the stawberry shortcake: Cut the cake in half. Top the bottom layer with half the strawberries, then replace the top of the cake. Top the cake with the remaining berries and whipped cream.
